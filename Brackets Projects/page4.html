Pyraminx guide

  1. orient all corners with their center pieces.
  2. Then pick a color and orient all center pieces and their tips on the same side.
  3. Then find all the pieces missing to complete the side. Rotate one of the bottom sides up, slot the piece in by rotating the top, and rotate the bottom side back down.
  4. Now you have finished the bottom layer. Use these algorithms to cycle until complete! Clockwise cycle: U’ F U F’ Counterclockwise cycle: U L’ U’ L Flip tow edges: F U’ F’ U F’ L F L’
